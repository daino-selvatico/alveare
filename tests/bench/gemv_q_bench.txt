-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Notes:
- The measured times include Host-to-Device streaming and PyXRT queue submission overheads (estimated at ~95 ms per 2048-row chunk execution).
- Raw NPU Estimation is calculated by subtracting the ~95 ms PyXRT overhead per chunk (2048 rows).
  - For 2048x2048 (1 chunk): Raw NPU = 107.67 ms - 95 ms = 12.67 ms (vs 572.21 ms Scalar and 108.46 ms Initial Vectorized).
  - For 2560x2560 (2 chunks): Raw NPU = 207.60 ms - 190 ms = 17.60 ms (vs 885.57 ms Scalar and 167.37 ms Initial Vectorized).
- Replacing the scalar type conversion loop with `aie::to_float<bfloat16>` vector intrinsics yields:
  - An additional ~8.5x to ~9.5x raw speedup over the initial vectorization attempt.
  - A total of ~45x to ~50x raw speedup over the original scalar implementation.
- End-to-End Llama-3.2-1B-Instruct generation latency improved from ~176.5 s/token (Scalar) and ~35.3 s/token (Initial Vectorized) to ~2.34 s/token (Optimized Vectorized), achieving a 75.4x overall speedup.
